## 📊 PART 8: CONTENT TONE & VOICE GUIDELINES

### Overall Tone
✅ **Professional** - Credible, knowledgeable, authoritative
✅ **Warm & Friendly** - Approachable, caring, supportive
✅ **Clear & Concise** - Easy to understand, no jargon
✅ **Inspiring** - Motivating, hopeful, empowering
✅ **Inclusive** - Welcoming to all, diversity-aware

### Writing Guidelines
- Use active voice ("We develop leaders" not "Leaders are developed")
- Short sentences & paragraphs for readability
- Avoid educational jargon
- Use "we," "us," "our" to build community
- Focus on student outcomes & benefits
- Specific examples over generalizations
- Address parent concerns directly
- Celebrate student achievements

### Content Length Guidelines
- Headlines: 6-8 words
- Subheadings: 8-12 words
- Paragraph: 40-60 words max
- Page intro: 100-150 words
- Full page sections: 300-500 words
